<h1>Final Simulations and Report Writing</h1>

With earlier work to compare our simulations to we could now do the final simulations. For each scenario we wanted to compare, we chose as many balls as we needed, tweaked the initial positions, velocities of the balls and ran the simulations and documented the results as well as took screenshots. We also wanted to run an extra simulation of dropping a ball without an initial velocity to see if our bounce physics seemed to be realistic, or at least to be able to discuss it. In this simulation we only measured the return height, that is the peak height between bounce 1 and 2.

To finish of the report we incorporated all of the results and some screenshots of the final simulations. Finally we wrote the discussion as well as we could with the time we had left. This was not the plan but the method and implementation of the report took a lot longer than we thought.
